TSPHP Online Demo

Write your TSPHP code in the text area on the left hand side and click on "compile" * or press CTRL+G * once you are done.
 
Click here for further information and help

Once you have started the compilation process, information about it will be shown at the bottom including syntax and semantic errors. If no error was detected then the corresponding PHP code will be shown in the text box on the right hand side.

Use the examples as a quick help if you are not yet familiar with the syntax enhancements or have a look at the Language Reference Manual. This online demo shall give you a better idea what TSPHP is all about. Please have a look at Vision of TSPHP for further information. Do you like the idea? Would be great if you contribute to the project in some way.

Be aware that TSPHP is still in an early stage. It does not support any built-in functions of PHP yet. Thus you can operate only on own classes and call own functions. Have a look at Scope to see what is not yet supported by TSPHP - this will change of course and with your help even faster.

 
Thank you very much for your interest in TSPHP. We would like to know what you found strange/surprising/hampering etc. about TSPHP in order to improve it. Please take some time and write a feedback to rstoll@tutteli.ch or leave your feedback as post in TSPHP's google group. You can also help in other ways - basically every contribution is welcome
